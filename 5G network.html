5G Network

It is the 5th generation mobile network. It is a new global wireless standard after 1G,2G,3G&4G networks. 5G enables a new kind of network that is designed to connect virtually everyone and everything together including machines,objects and devices. 5G wireless technology is meant to deliver hugher multi-Gbps peak data speeds. Higher performance and improved efficiency empower new user experiences and connects a new industries. 5G is designed to provide more connectivity than was ever available before.
